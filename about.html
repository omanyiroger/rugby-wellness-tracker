About the Rugby Application

This rugby application is designed to support players, coaches, and support staff by streamlining key aspects of player management, injury tracking, and performance monitoring. Whether it's managing player profiles, logging injuries, tracking rehab progress, or organizing training data, the system provides a centralized, easy-to-use platform built specifically for the demands of rugby.

Key features include:

  • Player Database Management: Store and update detailed player profiles and stats.
  • Injury & Rehab Logging: Track injuries, medical assessments, and rehabilitation progress in real-time.
  • Performance Insights: Monitor player performance and workload for better decision-making.
  • Match & Training Integration: Connect player data with match performance and training attendance.
  • Secure & Scalable: Built with data integrity and future expansion in mind.

Developed with input from rugby athletes and medical professionals, this application bridges the gap between on-field performance and off-field care, ensuring every player gets the support they need to stay fit, perform at their best, and return stronger.
